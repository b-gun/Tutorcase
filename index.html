Who are we?

Tutorcase is a education focused start up focusing on making education more accessible to everyone.

We're trying to flip the classroom and let students receive tuition from home at the time that suits him or her.

Instead of going to classes after school, Tutorcase will make it so that the average student can go home and receive tuition at his or her own pace.

How?

The modern day student is an excellent multitasker, and our 'portal' UI is designed to reflect that.

The student will be able to watch a livestream of the tutor explaining the concepts behind each lesson.

While this is happening, the student can talk to the tutor and other students taking part in the lesson, to gain more knowledge and ask questions, while also being able to refer to his or her notes all on one screen.
